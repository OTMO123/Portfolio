Strategic Cloud Zones

Behind Cards

Background clouds float behind content providing atmospheric depth without interfering with readability. They create a sense of space and immersion.

Overlay Effects

Overlay clouds appear above content with reduced opacity and screen blend mode. They maintain text legibility while adding dynamic visual interest.

Organic Motion

Each cloud has unique floating patterns with gentle rotation and breathing effects. The motion feels natural and non-distracting.

Interactive Parallax

Mouse movement creates subtle parallax effects. Overlay clouds respond more dramatically than background clouds for depth perception.

Procedural Generation

Clouds are generated procedurally in your neo style with concentric patterns, organic variations, and floating particles.

Performance Optimized

Strategic placement ensures optimal performance. Cloud count and quality adapt based on device capabilities.
